Common questions about your diet plan.

How is my calorie goal calculated?

Your calorie goal is based on your BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) calculated from the weight, height, and age you provided, adjusted by your activity level modifier.

Why can't I see the recipes?

Recipes are generated only after you complete your initial profile setup. Make sure your "Diet Preferences" are fully saved.
